## EVALUATION FRAMEWORK

For each question-answer pair, assess **SIX dimensions**:

### 1️⃣ ANSWER CORRECTNESS (0-10 points)
**What to assess:**
- Factual accuracy against the source document
- Correct interpretation of regulatory requirements
- No misstatements or incorrect claims

**Scoring:**
- 10: Completely accurate
- 7-9: Mostly accurate, minor imprecisions
- 4-6: Partially correct, contains errors
- 1-3: Mostly incorrect
- 0: Completely wrong

### 2️⃣ GROUNDEDNESS / FAITHFULNESS (0-10 points) ⚠️ MOST CRITICAL
**What to assess:**
- Is EVERY claim supported by the retrieved chunks?
- Does the answer introduce information NOT in the context?
- Are there hallucinations (fabricated facts, sections, citations)?

**Scoring:**
- 10: 100% grounded, every claim traceable
- 7-9: Mostly grounded, minor unsupported details
- 4-6: Significant unsupported claims
- 1-3: Mostly hallucinated
- 0: Completely fabricated

**CRITICAL:** Flag ANY hallucination. This is the primary RAG failure mode.

### 3️⃣ RETRIEVAL QUALITY (0-10 points)
**What to assess:**
- Do retrieved chunks contain information needed to answer?
- Are the TOP chunks actually the most relevant?
- Would different sections have been better?

**Scoring:**
- 10: Perfect retrieval, top chunk directly answers question
- 7-9: Good retrieval, answer in top-3 chunks
- 4-6: Partial retrieval, some relevant info missing
- 1-3: Poor retrieval, barely relevant
- 0: Complete retrieval failure

### 4️⃣ SOURCE ATTRIBUTION (0-10 points)
**What to assess:**
- Are sources cited with section numbers/titles?
- Is attribution accurate (document, section, page)?
- Can claims be traced to specific chunks?

**Scoring:**
- 10: Precise citations, fully traceable
- 7-9: Good citations, minor gaps
- 4-6: Vague or partially incorrect
- 1-3: Poor or missing citations
- 0: No attribution

### 5️⃣ COMPLETENESS (0-10 points)
**What to assess:**
- Does the answer address ALL parts of the question?
- Is critical information missing that exists in chunks?
- Is detail level appropriate (not too brief/verbose)?

**Scoring:**
- 10: Complete, addresses all aspects
- 7-9: Mostly complete, minor gaps
- 4-6: Incomplete, missing significant aspects
- 1-3: Barely addresses question
- 0: Does not answer question

### 6️⃣ CONTEXT SUFFICIENCY (YES / NO / PARTIAL)
**What to assess:**
- If you had ONLY the retrieved chunks (no full PDF), could you verify the answer?

**Scoring:**
- YES: Chunks contain sufficient information
- PARTIAL: Chunks contain some but not all needed info
- NO: Chunks insufficient, need full document
